Sprucing up a proposal

- Don't trumpet current weakness. Eg "As an underresourced nonprofit"
- Get specific: "We would staff this side" -> "We want to invest in a dedicated staffer"
     Or "strong results" -> "save up to ten minutes per conversation"
     Or "semi-automated services and lightweight supervision, to hands-off content and courses"
- Show clear action: "Our early work in AI" -> "We're already deploying AI tools to make human counselors more efficient"
- Simplify where the contextual implication doesn't need elaboration(?): "developing countries reeling from dramatic cuts in international aid" -> "as agencies suffer funding cuts"
- "Privacy" section... moving from the specific to the general... when it's boring? Eg, HIPAA, SOC2 -> "Aselo makes it easier for nonprofits to meet both regulatory requirements... and their ethical obligations"
